About Us

Welcome to DreamHash

DreamHash is registered in the UK as "DREAMHASH CLOUD MINING TECHNOLOGIES, LTD". Our company was formed to utilize advanced cloud mining technology to mine for Cryptocurrencies.

Cloud mining enables our company to earn Bitcoins without bitcoin mining hardware, mining software, electricity, or bandwidth. As our mining is done remotely on the cloud, this means we do not need to deal with any of the troubles usually encountered when mining Bitcoins such as electricity costs, overheating, installing hardware, or problems with hardware upkeep.

We have made it simple to earn money through mining Bitcoin, without needing to learn about the technical details involved. All you need to do is deposit your Bitcoins, and watch them grow every day!
